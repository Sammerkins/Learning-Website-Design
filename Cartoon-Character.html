Wonder Woman

About Wonder Woman

Wonder Woman's official title is Princess Diana of Themyscira. She was raised on the hidden island of Themyscira,
also known as Paradise Island. Diana is an Amazon, like the figures of Greek legend.
Orginally written in the 1940's Wonder Woman was depicted as fighting the axis millitary forces, contradicting the damsles in distress theme of the era. As a member of the Justus League, she stands for truth, justice and equality to people everywhere.

Characteristics

  1. She's a super hero
  2. She has weapons like her lasso of truth and indestructable bracelets,
  3. She is an Amazon
